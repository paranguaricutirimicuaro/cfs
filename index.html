Science Behind Us

Water condensate happens within all air conditioner units, this happens because the A/C traps heat and moisture from the enviorment, after a while these two mix and condensate into water, this is called ditilation. Distilled water is the purest form of H2O currently available, distilation is the same process that chemist use when seperating substances from eachother.

About CFS

Our product connects to an Air Conditioner through a tube in a way that all water being condensed inside the AC gets into the tank to be stored, instead of just being thrown away. This tank has a faucet for obtaining the water inside of it and is also connected to a hose with the idea to water plants and green ceilings.

Product Use

Install

Attach the tube to the tank and install it to the Air Conditioner unit

Wait

Stand by until the water inside the air coniditioner starts the process of condensation by getting in contact of its cold surface

Collect

Depending on your unit's capacity, the tank will collect more or less water. A one tonne AC will generate up to 10L of water in about 7-8hrs

Use

In order to use the water inside the tank, you can either open the faucet to directly collect the water or use the hose to water plants
